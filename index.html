The meeting of the Tapajós and Amazon rivers 🌊

Places Food Tips

🌴 Must-See Places

Alter do Chão

Known as the Amazonian Caribbean, with crystal-clear waters and white sand.

Meeting of the Waters

See the Tapajós and Amazon rivers flowing side by side without mixing.

Santarém Waterfront

Perfect for walking at sunset and trying local food.

🍛 Flavors of the Amazon

Don't miss trying:

  • Tacacá
  • Duck in tucupi sauce
  • Grilled fish (tambaqui and pirarucu)
  • Regional açaí (very different from the south!)

💡 Tips for Visitors

  • Best time: Amazon summer (less rain)
  • Always use sunscreen ☀️
  • Bring cash for small businesses
